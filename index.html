Hi, my name is Sebastian Elias and I'm a designer based in Austin, TX.

Design is how I tell stories. Whether I'm designing soundscapes or websites, the intention behind the content is what gives it meaning. When I make things for others, it's important for me to know my audience, so I can give my work proper meaning. This wis where UX comes into play. With an academic background in research psychology and an informal education in graphic arts, discovering UX/UI design has allowed me to combine my creative and interpersonal skills to tell the rich and varied stories that clients need.
